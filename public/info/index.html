About

Zach Krall (American, b. 1993 Japan) is a visual artist and creative technologist using images, video, and code to explore a contemporary understanding of the human condition.

You can download his public encryption key on Keybase and join his mailing list through Tinyletter.

His email is zach@zachkrall.com but you may just want to Tweet at him.
